Creating a Minity link:

Calling Method: POST

Call To: https://minity.link/api/create

Content Type: application/x-www-form-urlencoded

Keys: {'url':'https://example.com'}

Get minity global statistics:

Calling Method: GET

Call To: https://minity.link/api/statistics
