About Minity

Over the past decade trust in URL shorteners have gone down hill, reason being.. users have no easy way to check where the shortened URL goes. To solve that problem in minity we use Embeds that show where your URL actually goes, no more worrying that the URL you just clicked goes to some malicious domain. We currently support URL embeds for popular the most popular metatag's for embeds such as: Open Graph Protocols & Twitter Cards So in short most modern social media sites such as twitter, slack and discord etc.. will render these embeds!
